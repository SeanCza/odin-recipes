Mac n' Cheese

Mac n' Cheese freshly baked

Mac n' Cheese is a classic comfort food. Consiting of a bechamel cheese sauce and short noodles. Typically baked in the oven for a crispy crust.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups scooby doo noodles
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup mozzarella
  • 1 cup cheddar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Bring water to a boil
  3. Make sauce
    1. Make roux
    2. Slowly add milk to roux
    3. Add cheese into bechamel sauce
  4. Cook pasta and strain pasta
  5. Mix sauce into pasta pot
  6. transfer to casserole dish
  7. Cover with cheese and breadcrumbs and bake until golden brown crust
