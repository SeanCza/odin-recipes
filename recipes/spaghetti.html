Spaghetti bolognese

Platted Spaghetti

A traditional italian dish comprised of a tomato based meat sauce over spaghetti noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 onion minced
  • 1 can of tomato paste

Steps

  1. Bring water a boil
  2. While bringing water to a boil, cook ground beef until brown
  3. Add noodles to water once boiling
  4. Mix spaghetti sauce with cooked beef
  5. Strain spaghetti noodles and save 2 cups of pasta water
  6. Mix pasta water with sauce until desired consistency
  7. Plate noodles and pour sauce over
  8. Enjoy!
